# iOS and Android Standard App Icons Reference

This reference documents the design language and visual characteristics of built-in system apps on iOS and Android platforms. Use these as design inspiration for similar app categories.

---

## iOS Standard Apps

### Communication

#### Messages (メッセージ)
- **Primary Color**: Green (#34C759 / #30D158)
- **Icon Elements**: Speech bubble
- **Style**: Solid green background, white speech bubble
- **Design Notes**: Simple, universally understood symbol for messaging

#### Phone (電話)
- **Primary Color**: Green (#34C759)
- **Icon Elements**: Phone handset
- **Style**: White handset icon on green gradient
- **Design Notes**: Classic telephone symbol, tilted for dynamism

#### FaceTime
- **Primary Color**: Green (#34C759)
- **Icon Elements**: Video camera
- **Style**: White camera icon on green background
- **Design Notes**: Video indicator differentiates from Phone app

#### Mail (メール)
- **Primary Color**: Blue (#007AFF)
- **Icon Elements**: Envelope
- **Style**: White envelope on blue gradient
- **Design Notes**: Traditional mail metaphor, clean lines

### Productivity

#### Calendar (カレンダー)
- **Primary Color**: White with red accent
- **Icon Elements**: Calendar page showing current date
- **Style**: Dynamic - displays actual current date
- **Design Notes**: Functional icon that updates daily, red for day of week

#### Notes (メモ)
- **Primary Color**: Yellow (#FFD60A)
- **Icon Elements**: Lined notepad with pen stroke
- **Style**: Yellow notepad background, handwritten-style line
- **Design Notes**: Skeuomorphic reference to paper notes

#### Reminders (リマインダー)
- **Primary Color**: White with colored circles
- **Icon Elements**: Checklist with colored bullet points
- **Style**: Clean list representation, multicolor accents
- **Design Notes**: Task completion visualization

#### Voice Memos (ボイスメモ)
- **Primary Color**: Black/Dark gradient
- **Icon Elements**: Audio waveform
- **Style**: White/red waveform on dark background
- **Design Notes**: Visual representation of sound recording

### Utilities

#### Clock (時計)
- **Primary Color**: Black
- **Icon Elements**: Analog clock face with moving hands
- **Style**: Dynamic - displays actual current time
- **Design Notes**: Functional real-time display, minimal design

#### Weather (天気)
- **Primary Color**: Blue gradient (sky colors)
- **Icon Elements**: Sun, clouds, dynamic weather
- **Style**: Illustrative, sometimes dynamic based on conditions
- **Design Notes**: Visual weather representation

#### Camera (カメラ)
- **Primary Color**: Gray/Silver
- **Icon Elements**: Camera lens/shutter
- **Style**: Realistic camera lens representation
- **Design Notes**: Instantly recognizable photography symbol

#### Photos (写真)
- **Primary Color**: Multicolor gradient (rainbow spectrum)
- **Icon Elements**: Stylized flower/pinwheel
- **Style**: Colorful petals representing photo diversity
- **Design Notes**: Vibrant, represents variety of memories

#### Maps (マップ)
- **Primary Color**: Multicolor (map representation)
- **Icon Elements**: Stylized road/intersection
- **Style**: Aerial view of roads with location marker
- **Design Notes**: Recognizable map fragment

#### Compass (コンパス)
- **Primary Color**: White/Gray with red accent
- **Icon Elements**: Compass needle
- **Style**: Traditional compass rose, red north indicator
- **Design Notes**: Navigation tool metaphor

#### Calculator (計算機)
- **Primary Color**: Black/Orange
- **Icon Elements**: Calculator buttons
- **Style**: Grid of calculator keys, orange for operations
- **Design Notes**: Functional representation of calculator

#### Settings (設定)
- **Primary Color**: Gray
- **Icon Elements**: Gear/cog
- **Style**: Mechanical gear with multiple teeth
- **Design Notes**: Universal settings/configuration symbol

### Media

#### Music (ミュージック)
- **Primary Color**: Red/Pink gradient (#FA2D48)
- **Icon Elements**: Musical note
- **Style**: White musical note on gradient background
- **Design Notes**: Classic music symbol, Apple Music brand

#### Podcasts (ポッドキャスト)
- **Primary Color**: Purple (#9B59B6)

- **Style**: Concentric broadcast waves
- **Design Notes**: Audio broadcast representation

#### TV (Apple TV)
- **Primary Color**: Black
- **Icon Elements**: Apple TV logo
- **Style**: Minimalist "tv" text or screen representation
- **Design Notes**: Clean, premium media branding

#### Books (ブック)
- **Primary Color**: Orange (#FF9500)
- **Icon Elements**: Open book
- **Style**: White book icon on orange background
- **Design Notes**: Reading/literature metaphor

### Files & Storage

#### Files (ファイル)
- **Primary Color**: Blue (#007AFF)
- **Icon Elements**: Folder
- **Style**: Blue folder icon
- **Design Notes**: Standard file management metaphor

## Design Patterns Comparison

### iOS Design Language
- **Style**: Clean, often with gradients
- **Colors**: Vibrant, saturated
- **Shapes**: Rounded squares (squircle)
- **Shadows**: Subtle, internal shading
- **Metaphors**: Often skeuomorphic origins

### Android/Material Design Language
- **Style**: Flat, geometric
- **Colors**: Google brand colors prevalent
- **Shapes**: Circles or adaptive shapes
- **Shadows**: Material elevation system
- **Metaphors**: Abstract, symbolic

### Common Icon Categories

| Category | iOS Style | Android Style |
|----------|-----------|---------------|
| Messaging | Solid speech bubble | Outlined/filled bubble |
| Phone | Tilted handset | Straight handset |
| Calendar | Dynamic date | Static "31" |
| Clock | Real-time hands | Static representation |
| Camera | Lens detail | Simplified lens |
| Settings | 3D gear | Flat gear |
| Photos | Flower/pinwheel | Pinwheel |
| Mail | Classic envelope | "M" envelope |

### Best Practices from System Icons
1. **Instant Recognition**: Category should be obvious at a glance
2. **Scalability**: Works at all sizes from 29px to 1024px
3. **Consistency**: Follows platform design language
4. **Simplicity**: Minimal elements, maximum clarity
5. **Meaningful Colors**: Colors reinforce function (green=communication, blue=productivity)
