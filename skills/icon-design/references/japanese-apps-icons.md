This reference catalogs iconic app designs from Japan's most popular applications, organized by category. Use these as design inspiration while ensuring originality.

## Payment & Finance Apps

- **Primary Color**: Red (#FF0033)
- **Icon Style**: Bold "P" letterform with speech bubble element
- **Design Notes**: High contrast, instantly recognizable, warm/energetic feeling

### LINE Pay
- **Primary Color**: LINE Green (#00B900)
- **Icon Style**: Integrated within LINE ecosystem branding
- **Design Notes**: Maintains LINE brand consistency with payment indicator
- **Association**: Trust through LINE messaging platform recognition

### Rakuten Pay (楽天ペイ)
- **Primary Color**: Rakuten Crimson (#BF0000)
- **Icon Style**: "R" symbol with Rakuten brand identity
- **Design Notes**: Part of Rakuten ecosystem, premium feel
- **Downloads**: 6.4+ million (2023)

### MerPay (メルペイ)
- **Primary Color**: Mercari Red
- **Icon Style**: Connected to Mercari marketplace branding
- **Design Notes**: Clean, minimal, modern Japanese aesthetic
- **Integration**: Seamless with Mercari flea market app

### d払い (d Barai)
- **Primary Color**: NTT Docomo Red/Magenta
- **Icon Style**: "d" letterform in brand colors
- **Design Notes**: Telecom brand recognition, tech-forward
- **Downloads**: 4.4+ million (2023)

## Design Patterns in Japanese App Icons

### Common Characteristics
1. **Simplicity**: Clean, minimal designs that scale well
2. **Bold Colors**: High saturation, often red or brand-specific
3. **Letterforms**: Initial letters as primary visual element
4. **White Space**: Effective use of negative space
5. **Rounded Elements**: Soft, approachable shapes

### Color Distribution
| Color | Category Examples |
|-------|------------------|
| Red | PayPay, Mercari, Yahoo Japan, Rakuten |
| Green | LINE, Google Maps, Spotify |
| Blue | SmartNews, Twitter/X |
| Black | TikTok, ZOZOTOWN |
| Orange | Food apps, News apps |
| Yellow | Piccoma, NewsPicks |

### Japanese Market Preferences
- **Information Density**: Japanese users accept detailed in-app interfaces
- **Icon Simplicity**: Prefer clean, minimal icons despite complex apps
- **Trust Signals**: Brand recognition is highly valued
- **Local Adaptation**: Global apps often maintain icon consistency
